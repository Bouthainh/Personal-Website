Full Stack - IoT Project: BARAKAH

Summary:
BARAKAH is an IoT-based system that simplifies food management using NFC tags, DHT sensors, and an ESP32 microcontroller.
It tracks expiration dates, monitors storage conditions, and prevents waste by sending timely IFTTT alerts.
With live data on ThingSpeak, BARAKAH makes food tracking smart and sustainable.
